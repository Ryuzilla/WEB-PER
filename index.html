FAQ

[ READ BY MACHINES ]
What is Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)?+
GEO is the practice of structuring content and data so generative AI models cite your brand inside their answers. Where SEO targets a ranked list of links, GEO targets the synthesized response itself — making your facts retrievable, trustworthy and quotable by ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini and Claude.
What is Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)?+
AEO optimizes content to be the single best answer to a question. It targets featured snippets, voice assistants and Google AI Overviews using clear question-and-answer structure, concise definitions and FAQ schema.
How is GEO different from traditional SEO?+
SEO optimizes for a ranked list of blue links. GEO optimizes for a single synthesized answer where there are no rankings — only whether the model cites you. GEO prioritizes machine-readable structure, factual clarity and authoritative sourcing over keyword density.
Which AI engines do you optimize for?+
ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google Gemini & AI Overviews, Microsoft Copilot, Claude and Grok — plus traditional Google and Bing organic search, which still feed the models.
How do you measure AI citations?+
We track citation share across answer engines, AI-referred traffic isolated from server logs, schema coverage, and the percentage of target prompts where your brand appears as a cited source.
